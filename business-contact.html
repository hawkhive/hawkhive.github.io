Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive my custom quote?

Our business team typically responds within 24 hours with a preliminary quote. For more complex requirements, we may need up to 48 hours to prepare a comprehensive proposal.

Is there a minimum volume requirement for business accounts?

We work with businesses of all sizes. While we offer the best pricing for volumes of 50+ packages per month, we can create customized solutions for businesses with lower volumes as well.

Can I integrate HawkHive with my e-commerce platform?

Yes! We offer API integration with popular e-commerce platforms including Shopify, WooCommerce, and Magento. Our technical team can assist with setup and integration.

What kind of reporting do you provide for business accounts?

We provide comprehensive monthly reports including delivery volume, costs, performance metrics, and carbon footprint analysis. You'll also have access to a dashboard with real-time analytics.

Are there any long-term contracts required?

No long-term contracts are required. We offer flexible month-to-month agreements with no cancellation fees. Our goal is to earn your business through excellent service, not through contracts.
