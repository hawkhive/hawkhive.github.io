About HawkHive Logistics

HawkHive Logistics Inc. is a modern hybrid delivery service designed for small and medium businesses. We understand that different shipments have different urgency levels.

That's why we offer flexible delivery options — from standard 1–3 day shipping to lightning-fast same-day service.

Our USP is ZEP (Zero-Error Priority) Same-Day Delivery, which ensures that if you schedule a pickup before 12:00 noon, your parcel will be delivered the same day within our corridor.

Currently, we operate along the Sarnia–Toronto corridor, covering all major cities in between (Sarnia, London, Woodstock, Kitchener, Hamilton, Mississauga, Toronto).
